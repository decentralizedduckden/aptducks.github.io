We are ...

A deflationary collection. Utilizing burning mechanisms, floor sweeping, and beautiful original duck art. The plan? Ducks to the moon!

The Dinner Plate

Utilizing 75% of SOL gathered from marketplace fees, the team plans on sweeping the duck floor, and burning it down. These ducks will then be removed from the total supply, and eaten, making our ducks DEFLATIONARY!

To show appreciation for our fallen duck comrades, the team will be cooking a specialized dinner plate, fueled by tears of the swept ducks. A timelapse of making the dish in real life, recording, publishing a professional, end-result photograph, and then photoshopping it to fit the specific ducks which had the misfortune of being swept and burned.

FAQ

Where do I get a duck?
Ducks will be available to mint on our launch date on TBD (Our minting link will be posted on twitter and discord 5 minutes before launch although we recommend using twitter as discord has a chance of crashing if too much traffic occurs.
How many ducks are in supply?
There are a total of 5000 initial ducks, however the team plans on utilizing our burning mechanism better known as the “dinner plate”. This will create a deflationary asset and further decrease the supply of ducks over time, thus incentivizing holders.
What is the mint cost?
The mint cost was heavily taken into consideration as the team wanted to make sure the price was in line to appeal to the most people possible, giving everyone a fair chance at mining!
What was the team thinking when they started the duck project?
As a team of four, we wanted to create a fun, sustainable, and determined atmosphere in which NFT lovers can gather together as a community and strengthen the love we all have for art.
Why ducks?
Funnily enough, the team first decided on doing penguin art but we soon discovered that ducks are cuter… and can actually fly.
Will it be hard to mint?
We will have anti bot protection by utilizing google captcha. You also have the opportunity to get a Presale spot via the discord ((by inviting 3 verified members (#presale-ticket-verify)) There will only ever be 500 presale spots available.
What are the future plans?
Being that this is our first project, we as developers and artists want to be more involved with the community. Our plans consist of events, new NFTs, and airdrops, and constantly looking for new ways to incentivize duck holders and original investors.
Is there a Pre-Sale?
The Pre-Sale is available for early duck lovers, as long as they invite three of their closest duck comrades into the discord. (as long as they verify themselves in #verify.)

We dearly hope you learned as much as you could about the duck project, the developers, our art, and our deep love for NFTs which we hope to share with you.
